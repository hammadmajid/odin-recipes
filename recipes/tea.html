Tea

Description

A fragrant and flavorful spiced tea that combines black tea with aromatic spices for a comforting and warming beverage.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 small piece of ginger (sliced)
  • 2 tsp sugar (or to taste)

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Add the cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, cloves, and ginger to the boiling water.
  3. Let the spices simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add the black tea leaves and let it steep for another 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the milk and bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Add sugar and stir until it dissolves.
  7. Strain the tea into cups and serve hot.
